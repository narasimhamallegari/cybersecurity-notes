## Data Classification (most to least sensitive)

Restricted: Legal, medical, and financial records. Strictest controls. Need-to-know only.
Confidential: Business plans, personnel records, customer data. Limited employees.
Internal: Policy documents, operational procedures. Employees only.
Public: Marketing materials, press releases. Freely shareable.

## Privacy Key Terms

PII: Personally Identifiable Information — name, SSN, address, DOB, email, phone, biometrics.
PHI: Protected Health Information — medical records, diagnoses, treatment history. Covered by HIPAA.
Data minimization: Only collect what you actually need.
Right to erasure (GDPR): Individuals can request deletion of their personal data.

## Security Policies

AUP (Acceptable Use Policy): What employees can do with company systems. Signed by all employees.
Password Policy: Minimum length, complexity, rotation period, history.
BYOD: Rules for personal devices on corporate network. MDM enrollment required.
Clean Desk Policy: No sensitive documents visible when away from the desk.
